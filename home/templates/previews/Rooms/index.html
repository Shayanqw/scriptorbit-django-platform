Sip, Savor & Sound
All in One Place

We are a listening bar, coffee shop, and casual drinking spot. Enjoy great coffee and music from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM every day, including holidays. Our bar is open throughout the week but remains closed on Tuesdays. Come by for a relaxed drink and good vibes.
