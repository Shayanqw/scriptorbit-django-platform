Welcome To Rosa's

Delicious Food & Cozy Atmosphere

Welcome to Rosa’s – sister to Atomic 10, where Latin charm meets modern elegance in downtown Toronto. Savor our contemporary take on Italian classics—savory pastas, fresh seafood, and indulgent desserts. Pair your meal with handcrafted cocktails and select wines for an unforgettable experience. With chic surroundings and impeccable service, every visit is a feast of flavor and sophistication.

Rosa's is modern Latin tapas restaurant in Corso Italia, and the sister spot to Atomic 10. Our chefs bring the vibrant flavors of Argentina, Colombia, Ecuador, and Mexico to life with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Enjoy beloved classics like ceviche and empanadas, reimagined with creative twists.
