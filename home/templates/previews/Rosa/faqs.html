What type of cuisine does Rosa's serve?

Rosa's specializes in modern Latin tapas, drawing inspiration from countries like Argentina, Colombia, Ecuador, and Mexico.

What are the operating hours of Rosa's?

Tuesday to Thursday: 4 PM – 11 PM; Friday: 4 PM – 12:30 AM; Saturday: 10 AM – 12:30 AM; Sunday: 10 AM – 10 PM; Closed on Mondays.

Does Rosa's offer vegetarian, vegan, halal or gluten-free options?

Yes, the menu includes vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free and some halal-friendly dishes.

Is there parking available at Rosa's?

Street parking is available near the restaurant.

Does Rosa's take reservations?

Yes, reservations can be made through our website or via platforms like OpenTable.

Is there live music or entertainment at Rosa's?

Rosa's features a DJ every Friday and Saturday night.

Does Rosa's offer takeout or delivery services?

Yes, takeout is available, and delivery can be ordered through platforms like SkipTheDishes.

Does Rosa’s have a bar or serve alcohol?

Yes, Rosa’s has a full bar with Latin-inspired cocktails, wines, and craft beers.
